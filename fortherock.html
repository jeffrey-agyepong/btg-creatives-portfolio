FOR THE ROCK

For The Rock is a music based ministry that promotes artists who point the world to Christ. Along with basic promotion shout outs via our Instagram Feed or Youtube Channel, FTR offers affordable artwork for Album, EP or Single covers.
Christopher Adam, the man behind For The Rock, aims to offer bands and musical artists high quality artwork to better further their reach for the Kingdom.
