About Us

This site is an extension of Beyond The Grave. BTG started to promote those at the forefront of the underground metal scene. Now extending that endeavor to those who work with bands behind the scenes.
Our goal is not to be a "middle man" but a way to connect artists/bands with creatives. Like the rest of our promotion, we do this all for free, but if you would like to support us, there are avenues to do so.
